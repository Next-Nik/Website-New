Appendix

Glossary.

The vocabulary of this ecosystem — defined plainly.

The ecosystem
Life OS
The personal navigation ecosystem. Five tools for seeing where you are across all of life and moving from that clarity. Foundation · Orienteering · Pulse · Purpose Piece · Horizon Leap.
NextUs
The civilisational coordination platform. A map of what humanity is working on across seven domains — and infrastructure for building toward a shared horizon.
The tools
Foundation
The nervous system entry point. A guided audio practice for regulation — the internal floor from which honest self-assessment becomes possible.
Orienteering
The directional navigation tool. A ten-minute honest assessment across all seven Life OS domains. Produces a map of where you actually are.
Pulse
The recurring rhythm tool. Daily check-in, weekly reflection, quarterly reset. Works at any stage — no prerequisites.
Purpose Piece
The contribution archetype tool. Surfaces the natural role you're built to play — the archetype, the domain, the scale at which your effort compounds most.
Horizon Leap
The full transformation programme. Facilitated by Nik. For people who keep hitting the same ceiling — identity-level work, not more tools.
Key concepts
The Seven Life OS Domains
Path · Spark · Body · Finances · Relationships · Inner Game · Outer Game.
The Seven NextUs Domains
Human Being · Society · Nature · Technology · Finance & Economy · Legacy · Vision.
Horizon Goals
A living picture of what 10/10 looks like in a given domain. Not a target to be mandated — a directional picture of flourishing that guides navigation.
Horizon Self
The version of you already living your Horizon Goals. A coherent identity to orient toward. Introduced inside Horizon Leap.
T.E.A.
Thoughts · Energy · Actions. The three lenses of the Pulse daily check-in.
The Fractal Principle
The same developmental physics that shape a single life also shape a civilisation. Life OS and NextUs use the same architecture at different scales — by design.
The Overview Effect
What astronauts report when they first see Earth from space — an irreversible recognition of the whole. The vantage point NextUs is built from.
